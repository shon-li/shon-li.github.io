就在这一刻，\\
一只来我窗台上避雨的小麻雀抖了抖翅膀，\\
发出一点可爱的声响，\\
飞走了。\\
<br>
<p lang="en">
    Right at this moment,
</p>
<p lang="en">
    a little sparrow came to my windowsill, sheltered from the rain, shook its wings.
</p>
<p lang="en">
    Flew away,
<br>
    with a lovely little sound.
</p>
